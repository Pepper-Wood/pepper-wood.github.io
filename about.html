About

B.S. Computer Science, Minor in Computer Systems Engineering

Kathryn DiPippo is a current senior at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute in Troy, NY, specializing in software development and digital art.

I am the type of student who finds joy in working on programming problems. I took basic programming classes in my later years at high school and realized my passion for programming after taking python programming and data structures my freshman year of college.

I spend my free time working on open-source projects and my second passion, digital art. One of my projects, Adoptapedia, ties both of these skills together by creating a directory for other artists to find places to submit their artwork.

I am highly self-motivated and a fast learner. Overall, my experience demonstrates my:
☀ Skill in prioritizing multiple, often competing, tasks
☀ Ability to meet challenges head on and resolve problems quickly
☀ Ability to streamline processes and create useful resources
